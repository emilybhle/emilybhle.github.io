RJ’S Food Rocks

Skills: Brand Development, Logo Design, Icon Creation
Programs: Adobe Illustrator

The YouTube channel, RJ’s Food Rocks, reached out to me to help rebrand their overall identity. Working with RJ was such a joy because he had a very clear vision of the channel’s visual and storytelling needs. Taking influence from Japanese Kawaii aesthetic and classic 80’s Memphis style motifs, we worked together to create a timeless, cute visual brand.

I enjoyed this project a lot because I got to work in new constraints by creating YouTube assets such as icons and banners.
