Emily Lê

Digital Design and Engagement



Hello and welcome! My name is Emily Lê and I am a visual storyteller.

I am a proud alum of Colorado State University where I earned my BFA in Electronic Art and am a soon-to-be graduate of Ryerson University’s Master of Digital Media (MDM) program in Toronto. Through my undergraduate degree I was able to create the backbone of my technical skills ranging from graphic design to video production. During my time with the MDM program, I was able to really expand my technical skills and delve deeper into design thinking, conceptualizing an experience, and advanced technologies.

My strengths lie within visual design, guest engagement, and creative marketing. My passion and work heavily lie within the themed entertainment industry. The creativity that stems from an environment that thrives off imagination and immersive play is the driving force of my inspiration and many of my projects. It has been a gift over these past few years to learn more about this industry and just how expansive it really is. I have been lucky enough to hold various roles within this industry including being a Safari Driver in Disney’s Animal Kingdom to working as the Communications Coordinator for a small theatre in the heart of Toronto. Please take a look around my portfolio and feel free to contact me for more information.
