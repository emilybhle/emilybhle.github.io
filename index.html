Emily Lê

Graphic Design, Visual Communications and Guest Engagement


Hello and welcome! My name is Emily Lê and I am a visual storyteller.

I am a proud alum of Colorado State University where I earned my BFA in Electronic Art and a graduate of Ryerson University’s Master of Digital Media (MDM) program in Toronto. Through my undergraduate degree I was able to create the backbone of my technical skills ranging from graphic design to video production. During my time with the MDM program, I was able to really expand my knowledge and dive deeper into design thinking, conceptualizing an experience, and advanced technologies.

My strengths lie within visual design, guest engagement, and creative marketing within the themed entertainment industry. The creativity that stems from an environment that thrives off imagination and immersive play is the driving force of my inspiration and many of my projects. It has been a gift over these past few years to learn more about this industry and just how expansive it really is. I have been lucky enough to hold various roles within this industry including working with Jack Rouse Associates as a Graphic Designer assisting with the concept design of an international theme park, creating the branding of many whimsical holiday experiences with NDM Hospitality, and currently as a Multimedia Designer with Disney Parks, Experiences, and Products.

I am also open for freelance, contract, and full-time opportunities.
